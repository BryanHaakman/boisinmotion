# Bois in Motion 5K Community Run

## Event Details

**When:** Saturday, February 15, 2025 at 9:00 AM  
**Where:** Queen's Park, Toronto, ON  
**Distance:** 5K (flexible pacing)  
**Cost:** Free  
**Currently Registered:** 187 / 250

## What to Expect

This is a low-pressure, high-fun community run. We'll gather at Queen's Park and run through some of downtown Toronto's best running routes. Whether you're a sub-20 minute 5K runner or taking it easy, there's a pace for you.

## Schedule

- **8:45 AM:** Arrive at Queen's Park (meetup spot: main entrance)
- **9:00 AM:** Group starts running
- **9:30 AM:** Finish and cool down
- **9:45 AM:** Head to local café for coffee and community chat

## What to Bring

- Running shoes (duh!)
- Water bottle
- Light layers
- Positive vibes

## Before You Go

- Check the weather and dress accordingly
- Let us know your name in Discord so we can spot you
- Tag #BoisInMotion on Instagram with your photos!

## Questions?

Drop a message in the #events channel on Discord or reply to our Instagram post.
